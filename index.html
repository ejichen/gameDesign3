Keep Your Shadow

by: I-Chen Jwo (ijwo)

Your shadow tries to run away from you, try to keep it from contacting other object's shadow.

the player is moving around on a circle planer surface and there are objects on the planar as well. Try to keep the player's shadow away from touching other objects and also try not to touch the objects' shadow. For example, if the sun goes down a little bit further, the player's shadow might touch obj1 and the shadow of obj2 might touch the player. That will let your shadow run away!

Gameplay

The sun will move around either orderly or randomly, that's up to the choice of the implementer. Could use WASD keys to move around the viewpoint and the arrow keys to control the player. The player wins higher score by keeping the shadow for a longer time.

Assets

Could use the old assets for the objects or use anything else instead.
