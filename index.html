Keep Your Shadow

by: I-Chen Jwo (ijwo)

Your shadow tries to run away from you, try to keep it from contacting other object's shadow.

The player is moving around on a circle planer surface and there are objects on the planar as well. Try to keep the player's shadow away from touching other objects and also try not to touch the objects' shadow. For example, if the sun goes down a little bit furthur, the playr's shadow might touch obj1 and the shadow of obj2 might touch the player. That will let your shadow run away!

Gameplay

The sun will move around either orderly or randomly, that's up the the choice of implementer. Could use WASD key to move around the view point and the arrow keys to control the player.
